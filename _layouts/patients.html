Take Control

Home infusion therapy works best when patients are well equipped to manage their care effectively in the absence of health practitioners. Infusion Connect puts patients in a much better position to troubleshoot problems with their infusion, keep track of infusion pump information and stay on top of their therapy all together.

Optimal Communication with your Nurse

Patients are able to reach their assigned nurse via text, phone call or live video chat, as long as the nurse is online. Otherwise, regular and after hours contacts for their infusion providers are on hand.
